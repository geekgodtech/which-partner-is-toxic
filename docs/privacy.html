Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2025

AIRTA Corp ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AIRTA – AI Relationship Analyzer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

SMS and Message Data (Android): With your explicit permission, the App reads SMS and RCS messages stored on your device solely for the purpose of performing the analysis you request. This data is never stored on our servers. It is transmitted directly to our AI provider (DeepSeek) for analysis and is discarded immediately after your report is generated.

Screenshots (iOS): On iOS, you voluntarily provide screenshots of conversations from your device's photo library. These images are processed on-device using Google ML Kit OCR to extract text. The extracted text is then transmitted to our AI provider for analysis and is not stored.

In-App Purchase Data: Transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics (e.g., crash reports) through Firebase to improve app performance. This data cannot be used to identify you.

API Key (Optional): If you provide your own DeepSeek API key, it is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To perform AI-powered relationship analysis at your explicit request
  • To deliver your report within the App
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription tier
  • To improve app stability and performance via anonymous analytics

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • DeepSeek AI — for processing your analysis request over encrypted connections. Data is not retained beyond the API request.
  • Google Firebase — for anonymous crash reporting and remote configuration only.
  • Apple / Google — for payment processing via their respective in-app purchase systems.

4. Data Retention

  • Conversation data: Never stored. Processed in real time and discarded.
  • Reports: Stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your generated reports.
  • Preferences and subscription status: Stored locally on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

AIRTA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted API communication (HTTPS/TLS). Since conversation data is never stored on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal conversations is minimized.
