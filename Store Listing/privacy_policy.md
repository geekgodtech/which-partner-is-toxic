# Privacy Policy — AIRTA: AI Relationship Analyzer

**Last Updated:** June 7, 2025
**Effective Date:** June 7, 2025

**AIRTA Corp** ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AIRTA – AI Relationship Analyzer mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) SMS and Message Data (Android)
With your explicit permission, the App reads SMS and RCS messages stored on your device solely for the purpose of performing the analysis you request. **This data is never stored on our servers.** It is transmitted directly to our AI provider (DeepSeek) for analysis and is discarded immediately after your report is generated.

### b) Screenshots (iOS)
On iOS, you voluntarily provide screenshots of conversations from your device's photo library. These images are processed on-device using Google ML Kit OCR to extract text. The extracted text is then transmitted to our AI provider for analysis and is not stored.

### c) In-App Purchase Data
When you make a purchase, transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information.

### d) Usage Data
We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics (e.g., crash reports, feature usage frequency) through Firebase to improve app performance. This data cannot be used to identify you.

### e) API Key (Optional)
If you provide your own DeepSeek API key, it is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To perform AI-powered relationship analysis at your explicit request
- To deliver your report within the App
- To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription tier
- To improve app stability and performance via anonymous analytics

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share data only with:
- **DeepSeek AI** — for processing your analysis request. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and is not retained by DeepSeek beyond the scope of the API request.
- **Google Firebase** — for anonymous crash reporting and remote configuration only.
- **Apple / Google** — for payment processing via their respective in-app purchase systems.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Conversation data**: Never stored. Processed in real time and discarded.
- **Reports**: Stored locally on your device only. We have no access to your generated reports.
- **Preferences and subscription status**: Stored locally on your device.

---

## 5. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Read SMS (Android) | To import conversations for analysis |
| Photo Library (iOS) | To import conversation screenshots |
| Contacts | To identify conversation participants by name |
| Storage | To save and share PDF reports |
| Internet | To communicate with DeepSeek AI API |

You may deny any permission. Denying SMS or photo access will limit the App's ability to import conversations automatically.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

AIRTA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted API communication (HTTPS/TLS). Since conversation data is never stored on our servers, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal conversations is minimized.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics collection

Since we do not store conversation data, most data associated with your use of the App exists only on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.
