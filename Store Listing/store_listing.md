# Store Listing — AIRTA: AI Relationship Toxicity Analyzer

## App Name

## Short Description *(80 chars — Play Store)*
> AI-powered analysis of your relationship conversations. Know the truth.

## Full Description *(Play Store & App Store)*

> **Is your relationship healthy — or are you missing the signs?**
>
> AIRTA uses advanced AI to analyze real conversations from your SMS, RCS, or chat history and generates a detailed psychological report on the communication patterns, emotional dynamics, and toxicity indicators present in your relationship.
>
> **How it works:**
> • Import your SMS/RCS conversations directly (Android) or via screenshots (iOS)
> • Select the psychological metrics you want analyzed — or let AIRTA choose 20 at random
> • AI generates a full forensic-style report with an executive summary, metric scores, contextual evidence, and a final conclusion
> • Download or share your report as a PDF
>
> **What AIRTA analyzes:**
> • Emotional manipulation & gaslighting patterns
> • Communication imbalance & stonewalling
> • Toxicity indicators across dozens of psychological metrics
> • Individual behavioral profiles for both partners
> • Neutral synthesis and final verdict
>
> **Who it's for:**
> • Anyone questioning the health of a relationship
> • People recovering from toxic or abusive relationships
> • Therapists and counselors wanting a client conversation snapshot
> • Researchers and psychology enthusiasts
>
> **Privacy first:** All analysis is processed through AI — your conversations are never stored on our servers.
>
> Available in 16 languages.
